The listener is running.

It starts automatically every time you log in, and receives 1-on-1 transcripts your iPhone pushes over your local network.

Pair your iPhone (one time):

  1. Open Terminal and run: cat ~/Luxicon/.sync-token
  2. In Luxicon on your iPhone: My Voice → Mac sync, enter that token.
  3. Optionally turn on Push automatically after each 1-on-1.

Transcripts land in the Luxicon folder in your home folder. To remove the listener later, run luxicon-listener-uninstall in Terminal.
